Environment

You act in a closed ring system with several participants, having a predecessor and a successor. The number of participants in the ring is unknown to you.

Situation

At every moment, you can request one unit of a virtual good from your predecessor. You collect a unit only if your predecessor has one in their inventory. Simultaneously, your successor can request a unit from you.

Inventory

Your inventory stores your units; it can be zero or positive.

  • You start with two units in your inventory.
  • Every time you collect a unit from your predecessor, your inventory increases by one.
  • Every time your successor collects a unit from you, your inventory decreases by one.

Your Actions

To request a unit, click REQUEST in the left box. When you collect it, the box turns green for a second; otherwise, it turns red. Your inventory adjusts accordingly.

Your Successor's action

Your successorâ€™s request has the same effect on the right box. When he/she collects it, the box turns green for a second; otherwise, it turns red.

Costs and Benefits

  • You start with an endowment of 30 ECUs.
  • Every time your successor collects a unit from you, you earn 10 ECUs.
  • Every second, you must pay 1 ECU for each unit in your inventory.
  • Your ECU balance adjusts accordingly. Technically, it can go negative, but you can also recover.

Round

A round ends after five minutes = 300 seconds. You will have 8 separate rounds with a fixed group.

Training

To get comfortable with the situation, you have a two-minute round without payments.

Payment

We pay one round. A random draw determines which one. For that round the ECUs in your balance will be converted to Euros at a rate 50 ECUs = 1 Euro.
